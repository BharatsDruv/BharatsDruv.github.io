             What is IP Entrance TEST
 
                    Informatics Practices Entrance Test is an entrance examination of objective pattern
                    which is conducted for admission in Informatics Practices (I.P.) Subject in 11th Standard of Kendriya Vidyalaya Mandsaur.
                    It is based on logical Questoning which includes mental ability test of student. 
                   This dynamic nature is built to ensure the merit-based selection and genuineness of the entrance test.
